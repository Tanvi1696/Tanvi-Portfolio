About Me

Hi, I'm Tanvi, an Instructional Designer passionate about creating learner-centered digital content. After teaching for 2.5 years, I became deeply interested in the power of eLearning and LMS tools. That passion led me to transition into Instructional Design, blending my classroom experience with modern learning technologies.

Projects

Voltage Basics for Warehouse Workers

A bite-sized, 1-minute microlearning module designed to introduce voltage safety to frontline warehouse staff. Built using Articulate 360, the course uses simple visuals, clear narration, and scenario-based cues to ensure fast, accessible learning with minimal disruption to daily operations. Ideal for just-in-time training or onboarding contexts.

Tool used: Articulate 360

Audience: Warehouse and logistics staff

Goal: Promote electrical safety awareness in under 2 minutes

Duration: 1-2 Minutes

Essentials of Time Management for Remote Workers

This course helps remote professionals build effective time management habits tailored to flexible work environments. Through real-world examples and reflective questions, learners are guided to take control of their schedules, boost productivity, and create healthier work-from-home routines.

Audience: Remote and hybrid professionals

Goal: Productivity habits, time-blocking, managing distractions
